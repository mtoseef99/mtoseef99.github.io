Research Interests

My research interests span Transfer Learning, Biomedical Informatics, Computational Biology, and Data Science. My recent research focuses on topics such as large language models (LLMs) and multimodal models (MMLMs) to genomics and clinical data.

I am particularly interested in exploring electronic health records (EHRs) using large language models and transfer learning approaches, with the goal of developing interpretable, scalable, and generalizable models that can support clinical decision-making across diverse patient populations.
